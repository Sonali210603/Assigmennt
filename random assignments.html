What is Ramadan?

Ramadan is the ninth month of the Islamic lunar calendar. It is a month of fasting, prayer, reflection, and community for Muslims around the world.

Popular Ramadan Customs

  • Fasting from dawn to sunset
  • Increased prayer and reading of the Quran
  • Charity and helping those in need
